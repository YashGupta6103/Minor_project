Discover Madhya pradesh’s unique spice alchemy, where every ingredient transforms, combining to create an experience beyond flavor.


Pyaaz Ki Kachori

Ingredients:
Flour, onions, and spices.

Process:
"Stuff spiced onions into flour dough, deep fry."

Benefits:
"Crispy, spicy, and flavorful, rich in carbohydrates."


Chana Dal Parantha

Ingredients:
Wheat flour, chana dal, and spices.

Process:
"Stuff chana dal mixture into wheat dough, roll, and cook on tawa."

Benefits:
"High in protein and fiber, filling breakfast option."



Rajgira Puri

Ingredients:
Amaranth flour, potatoes, and spices.

Process:
"Knead dough with amaranth flour, roll into puris, deep fry."

Benefits:
"Gluten-free, rich in calcium and iron, suitable for fasting."

Biryani Pilaf

Ingredients:
Rice, meat or vegetables, saffron, and spices.

Process:
"Layer rice with spiced meat or vegetables, cook on slow heat."

Benefits:
"Aromatic and nutritious, rich in flavors and proteins."




Matar Pulao

Ingredients:
Rice, green peas, and whole spices.

Process:
"Cook rice with green peas and aromatic whole spices."

Benefits:
"Light and flavorful, high in carbohydrates and fiber."

Bhopali Gosht Korma

Ingredients:
Mutton, yogurt, onion, and spices.

Process:
"Cook mutton in a yogurt-based spiced gravy."

Benefits:
"Protein-rich and flavorful, hearty traditional dish."

Sabudana Vada

Ingredients:
Sago, peanuts, potatoes, and spices.

Process:
"Soak sago, mix with peanuts and potatoes, shape into vadas, and deep fry."

Benefits:
"High in carbohydrates and energy, perfect for fasting."

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:
Khoya (dried milk), sugar syrup, and cardamom.

Process:
"Deep fry khoya balls, soak in sugar syrup until sweet and soft."

Benefits:
"Rich dessert, provides instant energy."

Khatta Chana

Ingredients:
Black chickpeas, tamarind, and spices.

Process:
"Cook black chickpeas with tamarind and spices for a tangy dish."

Benefits:
"High in protein and fiber, aids digestion."

Jalebi

Ingredients:
Flour, sugar syrup, and saffron.

Process:
"Deep fry flour batter in spiral shapes, soak in sugar syrup."

Benefits:
"Sweet, crispy, and energizing, popular in festive seasons."

Methi Pulao

Ingredients:
Rice, fenugreek leaves, and spices.

Process:
"Cook rice with fresh fenugreek leaves and spices."

Benefits:
"Rich in iron and fiber, a flavorful and nutritious dish."

Bhutte Ke Ladoo

Ingredients:
Corn, jaggery, and ghee.

Process:
"Cook corn with jaggery and ghee, shape into ladoos."

Benefits:
"Sweet and energy-rich, unique to Madhya Pradesh."





Kesar Pista Kulfi

Ingredients:
Milk, saffron, and pistachios.

Process:
"Boil milk with saffron, add pistachios, freeze until set."

Benefits:
"Cooling and rich in calcium and energy."

Bhutte Ka Halwa

Ingredients:
Grated corn, milk, sugar, and ghee.

Process:
"Cook grated corn with milk and sugar, finishing with ghee."

Benefits:
"Rich in fiber and carbohydrates, a sweet and creamy dessert."





Mirchi Bhajiya

Ingredients:
Green chilies, gram flour, and spices.

Process:
" Coat green chilies with gram flour batter, deep fry until crispy."

Benefits:
"Spicy and crunchy, a popular street snack."
