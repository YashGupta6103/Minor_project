Enter the world of Odisha flavors where boldness meets balance, and each dish is a celebration of local pride.

Pakhala Bhata

Ingredients:
Cooked rice, water, yogurt, and green chilies.

Process:
"Ferment cooked rice in water and yogurt overnight."
"serve chilled with green chilies."

Benefits:
"Contains probiotics, aiding digestion and cooling the body."

Nadia Ghanta Tarkari

Ingredients:
Mixed vegetables, grated coconut, mustard paste, and spices.

Process:
"Cook vegetables with grated coconut and mustard paste for a hearty curry."

Benefits:
"High in fiber and essential nutrients, supporting overall health."

Khechudi

Ingredients:
Rice, moong dal, ghee, cumin seeds, and vegetables.

Process:
"Cook rice and dal with ghee, cumin seeds, and vegetables for a comforting one-pot meal."

Benefits:
"High in fiber and protein, easy to digest."



Jhuna Mansa Tarkari

Ingredients:
Mutton, mustard oil, onions, garlic, and spices.

Process:
"Marinate mutton, smoke it over charcoal, and cook with a spicy gravy."

Benefits:
"High in iron and protein, boosting energy levels."




Rasagola

Ingredients:
Chhena (fresh cottage cheese), sugar, and water.

Process:
"Shape chhena into balls and boil in sugar syrup until soft and spongy."

Benefits:
"A light dessert with calcium and protein."

Maccha Patra Poda

Ingredients:
Fish, mustard paste, turmeric leaves, and mustard oil.

Process:
"Marinate fish in spices, wrap in turmeric leaves, and grill until tender."

Benefits:
"Low in fat, rich in antioxidants from turmeric leaves."

Santula

Ingredients:
Raw banana, potato, brinjal, beans, ginger, cumin seeds, and milk.

Process:
"Cook mixed vegetables with ginger and cumin seeds, finished with a splash of milk."

Benefits:
"High in fiber and nutrients, supporting immunity and digestion."




Poi Chingudi

Ingredients:
Malabar spinach, prawns, green chilies, and mustard oil.

Process:
"Cook prawns and spinach together with spices for a flavorful curry."

Benefits:
"High in iron, calcium, and protein, good for bone and blood health."




Chitau Pitha

Ingredients:
Rice flour, coconut, jaggery, and milk.

Process:
"Mix rice flour with coconut and jaggery."
"cook in small rounds for a soft pancake."

Benefits:
"Provides energy and essential nutrients, good for digestion."



Palua Ladu

Ingredients:
Arrowroot powder, ghee, jaggery, and cardamom.

Process:
"Roast arrowroot with ghee and jaggery, then shape into laddus."

Benefits:
"Gluten-free and light, providing quick energy."

Ambila

Ingredients:
Mixed vegetables, tamarind, mustard seeds, and turmeric.

Process:
"Boil vegetables in a tamarind broth for a tangy, light soup."

Benefits:
"High in vitamins, good for digestion."


Chuda Kadamba

Ingredients:
Flattened rice, grated coconut, jaggery, and cardamom.

Process:
"Mix flattened rice with coconut and jaggery, shape into small laddus."

Benefits:
"Quick source of energy, rich in iron and fiber."

Poda Pitha

Ingredients:
Rice flour, jaggery, coconut, and spices.

Process:
"Mix rice flour with jaggery and coconut, then bake for a chewy cake."

Benefits:
"Provides energy and essential nutrients."



Manda Bhaja

Ingredients:
Rice flour, coconut, jaggery, and spices.

Process:
"Fill rice flour dough with coconut and jaggery, then deep-fry until crispy."

Benefits:
"Provides energy and is naturally gluten-free."





Poda Machha

Ingredients:
Fish, mustard paste, garlic, green chilies, and banana leaves.

Process:
"Marinate fish in mustard paste, wrap in banana leaves"
"grill for a smoky flavor."

Benefits:
"High in protein and omega-3, supporting heart health."
