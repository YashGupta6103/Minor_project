Our Bihar's Recipes capture the rich culinary traditions of the region, Perfect for lovers of bold tastes.

Chana Ghugni

Ingredients:
Black chickpeas, cumin seeds, ginger, turmeric, salt.

Process:
"Boil chickpeas, sauté cumin seeds, ginger, turmeric, and salt".
"Add boiled chickpeas and cook for a few minutes."

Benefits:
"High in protein and fiber, supports muscle health and digestion."

Aloo Puri

Ingredients:
Boiled potatoes, cumin seeds, turmeric, salt, wheat flour (for puri).

Process:
"Sauté cumin seeds, add mashed boiled potatoes, turmeric, and salt:"
"prepare dough with wheat flour and roll out for puris, then fry briefly."

Benefits:
"Provides energy and is a good source of carbohydrates and fiber."




Sattu Paratha

Ingredients:
Wheat flour, sattu, cumin, salt.

Process:
"Make dough with wheat flour and water."
"Mix sattu with cumin and salt."
"Stuff the dough with sattu mix and roll into flatbreads."

Benefits:
"Rich in fiber and protein, aids digestion and provides sustained energy."

Besan ka Cheela

Ingredients:
Gram flour (besan), water, cumin, salt.

Process:
"Mix gram flour with water, cumin, and salt to form a batter. Cook like a pancake on a pan."

Benefits:
"High in protein and fiber, supports muscle health and digestion."

Dahi Chura

Ingredients:
Flattened rice, curd, jaggery.

Process:
"Soak flattened rice for a minute, drain, and mix with curd and jaggery."

Benefits:
"High in calcium and probiotics, promotes gut health and provides quick energy."

Litti Chokha

Ingredients:
Whole wheat flour, sattu, spices, boiled brinjal, tomatoes, and potatoes.

Process:
"Make a dough with whole wheat flour, prepare stuffing with sattu and spices, bake briefly."
"Mash boiled vegetables together."

Benefits:
"Rich in protein, fiber, and vitamins, supports digestion and boosts energy."



Sooji Halwa

Ingredients:
Semolina, sugar, ghee, and water.

Process:
"Roast semolina in ghee, add water and sugar, and cook until thickened."

Benefits:
"Provides quick energy and is comforting."

Kachori

Ingredients:
Whole wheat flour, sattu, spices, and water.

Process:
"Make a dough, prepare a filling with sattu and spices, roll and cook briefly."

Benefits:
"Provides energy and a source of protein."





Pulao

Ingredients:
1 tomato, chopped, lemon juice
1 cup paneer cubes, 1 small cucumber, chopped

Process:
"Mix paneer with cucumber and tomato."
"Add lemon juice and serve."

Benefits:
"Protein-rich, refreshing."



Khaja

Ingredients:
Flour, sugar syrup, ghee.

Process:
"Make dough with flour and ghee."
"ry briefly, then dip in sugar syrup."

Benefits:
"High-calorie, provides instant energy, rich in carbs."

Aloo Tamatar Sabzi

Ingredients:
Boiled potatoes, tomatoes, cumin, turmeric, salt.

Process:
"Sauté cumin and tomatoes, add boiled potatoes and turmeric, cook for a few minutes."

Benefits:
"Rich in vitamins and antioxidants, supports heart health and boosts immunity."

Kheer

Ingredients:
Rice, milk, sugar, and cardamom.

Process:
"Cook rice in milk, add sugar and cardamom, simmer until creamy."

Benefits:
"Provides calcium and energy, supports bone health."




Jeera Chawal

Ingredients:
Rice, cumin seeds, and ghee.

Process:
"Cook rice with cumin seeds in ghee for flavor."

Benefits:
"Light and aromatic, easy to digest."


Phulka

Ingredients:
Whole wheat flour, water, and salt.

Process:
"Knead flour with water and salt."
"cook on a pan until puffed."

Benefits:
"High in fiber, low-calorie, easy to digest."





Sabudana Khichdi

Ingredients:
Soaked sabudana, peanuts, green chilies.

Process:
" Sauté soaked sabudana with peanuts and green chilies."

Benefits:
"Good for energy, high in carbs, ideal for fasting."
