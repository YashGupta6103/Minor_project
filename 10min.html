With our 10-Minute Service, you never have to sacrifice health for speed when it comes to your meals.


Vegetable Sandwich

Ingredients:
Bread, cucumber, tomato, onion
Green chutney, butter, salt, pepper

Process:
"Spread butter and chutney on bread."
"Add veggies, season, and close sandwich."

Benefits:
"Full of fiber and vitamins."


Poha

Ingredients:
1 cup flattened rice (poha)
1 small onion, green chili, curry leaves
Peanuts, turmeric, salt

Process:
"Rinse poha and drain."
"Sauté peanuts, onions, curry leaves, and turmeric."
"Mix in poha and cook for 2-3 mins."

Benefits:
"Light, iron-rich, easy to digest."

Bread Omelette

Ingredients:
2 eggs, 2 bread slices
Onion, tomato, green chili
Salt, pepper

Process:
"Mix eggs, veggies, salt, and pepper."
"Cook on a pan with bread slices on top."

Benefits:
"Protein-rich, quick energy from bread."

Besan Chilla

Process:

"Protein-packed, filling and healthy."
