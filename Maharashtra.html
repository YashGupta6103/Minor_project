Puran Poli

Ingredients:
Wheat flour, chana dal, jaggery, and ghee.

Process:
"Stuff wheat dough with sweetened chana dal mixture, roll, and cook on tawa."

Benefits:
"Rich in protein and energy, a festive sweet flatbread."

Pav Bhaji

Ingredients:
Mixed vegetables, pav, and spices.

Process:

"Rich in vitamins and fiber, a popular street food."

"Crispy and savory, a delicious snack."
