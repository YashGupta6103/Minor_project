Ingredients:

Process:
"Coat potato balls with besan batter, fry."

Benefits:
"Crispy and filling snack."

Garelu

Corn on the cob
Lemon, chili powder, salt

"Rub lemon, chili powder, and salt."

"High in fiber and antioxidants."



Cabbage Pakoda

Mudda Koora

Ingredients:
Mixed greens, green chili, garlic, salt.

Process:
"Boil mixed greens until soft, then mash with green chili and garlic."

Benefits:
"Full of essential vitamins and minerals, great for overall health."

Process:
"Sauté grated coconut with curry leaves and mustard seeds, then mix with cooked rice."

"Refreshing, aromatic, and rich in healthy fats."
