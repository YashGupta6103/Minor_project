Dhokla

Ingredients:
Gram flour, yogurt, turmeric, Eno.

Process:
"Ferment batter, steam until fluffy."

"Low-calorie, good for digestion."


Khandvi

Ingredients:

Process:
"Cook batter, roll into thin strips, temper with spices."

Benefits:
"High in protein, easy to digest."


Thepla

Benefits:
"Fiber-rich, controls blood sugar."

Fafda

Process:

Benefits:
"Protein-packed, filling snack."

Undhiyu

" Slow-cook vegetables and dumplings with spices."

Mohanthal

"Rich in protein, festive dessert."




Kansar

"Good for energy, traditional dessert."
