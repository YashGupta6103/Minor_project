Each recipe tells of Mizoram’s resilience, where cuisine adapts yet preserves the cherished flavors of its roots.

Chal Bai

Ingredients:
Pumpkin, rice, garlic, and green chilies.

Process:
"Simmer rice with pumpkin and spices for a mildly sweet and savory stew."

Benefits:
"High in fiber and vitamins, a soothing, nutritious meal."

Sawhchiar

Ingredients:
Rice, meat (chicken or pork), onions, and green chilies.

Process:
"Cook rice with meat and spices for a savory, filling pulao."

Benefits:
"High in protein and carbohydrates, an energy-rich meal."

Saum

Ingredients:
Fermented mustard leaves, garlic, and green chilies.

Process:
"Mix fermented mustard leaves with spices for a tangy salad."

Benefits:
"Probiotic-rich, aids digestion, and enhances the immune system."



Bai Ngampa

Ingredients:
Local vegetables, fish, bamboo shoots, and salt.

Process:
"Simmer vegetables with fish for a light, nutritious stew."

Benefits:
"High in protein and omega-3, a refreshing dish."



Bekang Bai

Ingredients:
Fermented soybean, vegetables (pumpkin, spinach), and green chilies.

Process:
"Boil fermented soybean with vegetables for a mildly tangy dish."

Benefits:
"Probiotic-rich, supports gut health."



Bai

Ingredients:
Mixed vegetables (like pumpkin, beans, spinach), bamboo shoots, and pork.

Process:
"Boil vegetables with bamboo shoots and pork for a light, flavorful stew."

Benefits:
"High in fiber and nutrients, promoting digestive health."

Arsa Buhchiar

Ingredients:
Chicken, rice, garlic, ginger, and green onions.

Process:
"Simmer rice with chicken and spices for a warm, hearty porridge."

Benefits:
"High in protein and easy to digest, perfect for cold days."


Khiang

Ingredients:
Rice flour, vegetable filling (like cabbage or carrots), and salt.

Process:
"Wrap rice flour around vegetable filling, steam until tender."

Benefits:
"A low-calorie snack, ideal for those seeking gluten-free options."

Chingal

Ingredients:
Sticky rice, banana leaves, and salt.

Process:
"Wrap sticky rice in banana leaves and steam for a fragrant treat."

Benefits:
"Gluten-free, easy to digest, and banana leaves add a natural flavor."

Pawt Na

Ingredients:
Fresh fish, local herbs, green chilies, and salt.

Process:
"Steam fish with herbs for a light, aromatic meal."

Benefits:
"High in omega-3, improves brain health, and herbs add antioxidants."

Arva Buh Chiar

Ingredients:
Sticky rice, meat broth (chicken or pork), and salt.

Process:
"Cook sticky rice in meat broth for a rich, hearty dish."

Benefits:
"High in protein and carbohydrates, ideal for energy boost."

Lai Kai

Ingredients:
Local herbs, beans, garlic, and green chilies.

Process:
"Boil beans with herbs for a hearty soup."

Benefits:
"High in protein and fiber, beneficial for immune health."

Dawl Fuan

Ingredients:
Boiled beans, chopped onions, green chilies, and lime juice.

Process:
"Toss beans with onions, chilies, and a squeeze of lime for a refreshing salad."

Benefits:
"Rich in fiber and protein, great for heart health."






An Tih

Ingredients:
Bamboo shoots, green chilies, garlic, and water.

Process:
"Boil bamboo shoots with garlic for a simple, refreshing soup."

Benefits:
"High in fiber, supports digestion and heart health."

Arsa Buh

Ingredients:
Chicken, sticky rice, garlic, and green chilies.

Process:
"Cook sticky rice with chicken for a comforting, protein-rich meal."

Benefits:
"Great for muscle repair, and rice adds energy."
