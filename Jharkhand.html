Get Food recipe of Jharkhand
from the Food Explorer.

Enter the world of Jharkhand flavors where boldness meets balance, and each dish is a celebration of local pride.




Malpua

Ingredients:
Wheat flour, sugar, fennel seeds, and milk.

Process:
"Make a batter with flour and milk, fry spoonfuls, and dip in sugar syrup."

Benefits:
"Sweet and delicious, often made during festivals."





Aloo Bharta

Ingredients:
Boiled potatoes, onions, green chilies, mustard oil.

Process:
"Mash potatoes with spices and mustard oil for flavor."

Benefits:
"Comfort food that’s simple, quick, and tasty."


Gur Soojni

Ingredients:
Wheat flour, jaggery, and ghee.

Process:
"Knead dough with jaggery, roll, and cook on a tawa."

Benefits:
"Sweet and energy-boosting, ideal for breakfast or snacks."


Marwa Ki Roti

Ingredients:
Finger millet flour, water, and salt.

Process:
"Knead millet flour into a dough, shape into rotis, and cook on a tawa."

Benefits:
"Gluten-free, rich in calcium, and good for bone health."

Tilkut

Ingredients:
Sesame seeds and jaggery.

Process:
"Roast sesame seeds, mix with jaggery, and shape into small balls."

Benefits:
"High in calcium and iron, provides warmth in winters."

Bada Pakhala

Ingredients:
Rice water, vegetables, mustard oil, and green chilies.

Process:
"Add chopped vegetables to rice water, season with mustard oil."

Benefits:
"Hydrating, rich in minerals, perfect for hot weather."

Paatra

Ingredients:
Colocasia leaves, gram flour, spices.

Process:
"Spread spiced gram flour on leaves, roll, steam, and fry."

Benefits:
"Iron-rich, savory snack with a crispy exterior."

Dumbi Curry

Ingredients:
Local fish, mustard oil, tomatoes, and spices.

Process:
"Cook fish in a spicy mustard-based curry."

Benefits:
"Rich in protein and omega-3, healthy and flavorful."

Peetha

Ingredients:
Rice flour, chana dal, spices.

Process:
"Stuff rice flour dumplings with spiced dal, steam."

Benefits:
"Protein-rich, light and filling, great as a snack or meal."

Rugda Curry

Ingredients:
Rugda mushrooms, onions, tomatoes, and spices.

Process:
"Sauté mushrooms with onions and spices, simmer until cooked."

Benefits:
"Unique to Jharkhand, rich in protein and antioxidants."

Murg Palak

Ingredients:
Chicken, spinach, onions, tomatoes, spices.

Process:
"Cook chicken with spinach and spices until tender."

Benefits:
"High in protein, iron-rich, and flavorful."


Kera Bharta

Ingredients:
Ripe bananas, mustard oil, green chilies, and salt.

Process:
"Mash bananas, mix with mustard oil, and season with green chilies."

Benefits:
"Rich in potassium, a quick and tangy dish."






Sukha Machhli

Ingredients:
Dried fish, onions, tomatoes, and spices.

Process:
"Soak, then cook dried fish with spices until tender."

Benefits:
"Rich in protein, popular among Jharkhand’s fishing communities."


Phutkal

Ingredients:
Pumpkin flowers, gram flour, and spices.

Process:
"Dip flowers in spiced gram flour batter, deep-fry until crispy."

Benefits:
"Unique, crunchy snack, high in fiber and vitamins."


Bhatua Saag

Ingredients:
Chenopodium greens, mustard oil, garlic, and spices.

Process:
"Sauté greens with mustard oil and garlic for a simple dish."

Benefits:
"High in vitamins and fiber, promotes good digestion."
