We bring you traditional Assam dishes, crafted for spice lovers. Experience the region’s unique taste with us.

Masor Tenga

Ingredients:
Fish, lemon, green chili.

Process:
"Fry fish; add lemon and chili, simmer briefly."

Benefits:
" Light and tangy; aids digestion."

Til Pitha

Ingredients:
Rice flour, sesame seeds, jaggery.

Process:
"Mix flour with water, fill with sesame and jaggery; cook on a griddle."

Benefits:
"Sweet snack; rich in iron."

Fried Potato Peels with Salt

Ingredients:
Potato peels, mustard oil, salt.

Process:
"Fry potato peels in mustard oil and sprinkle with salt."

Benefits:
"Reduces food waste, and is high in fiber."

Narikol Pitha

Ingredients:
Rice flour, grated coconut, jaggery.

Process:
"Mix coconut with jaggery; fill in rice flour batter and fry."

Benefits:
"Sweet and filling; high in fiber."

Baanhgajor Lagot Kukura

Ingredients:
Chicken, bamboo shoot, green chili.

Process:
"Cook chicken with bamboo shoot and green chili."

Benefits:
"High in protein; unique earthy flavor."

Hingara

Ingredients:
Gram flour, spices, salt.

Process:
" Mix ingredients into a batter; deep fry dumplings."

Benefits:
"Protein-rich; perfect savory snack."



Boiled Sweet Corn
with Chili Powder

Ingredients:
Sweet corn, chili powder, salt.

Process:
"Boil sweet corn and season with chili powder and salt."

Benefits:
"High in fiber and antioxidants, good for heart health."

Fried Drumstick Flowers

Ingredients:
Drumstick flowers, chickpea flour, salt.

Process:
"Coat flowers in flour and fry in oil."

Benefits:
"Rich in nutrients, especially Vitamin C."

Stir-Fried Lotus Stem with Green Chili

Ingredients:
Lotus stem, green chili, mustard oil.

Process:
"Sauté lotus stem slices with green chili in mustard oil."

Benefits:
"High in fiber, good for digestion."

Bilahir Anja

Ingredients:
Tomatoes, turmeric, salt, water.

Process:
"Boil tomatoes with turmeric and salt until they form a soupy consistency."

Benefits:
"High in antioxidants, good for skin health."

Assamese Lentil Soup

Ingredients:
Lentils (masoor or moong), turmeric, salt, water.

Process:
"Boil lentils with turmeric and salt until soft and soupy."

Benefits:
"High in protein and fiber, supports heart and digestive health."

Gandharaj Chicken

Ingredients:
Chicken, lemon juice, green chilies, salt.

Process:
"Boil chicken with lemon juice, green chilies, and salt until cooked."

Benefits:
"High in protein, promotes healthy skin and bones."

Moori Bhaat

Ingredients:
Puffed rice, cucumber, tomatoes, coriander, salt.

Process:
"Mix puffed rice with chopped vegetables and salt."

Benefits:
"Low in calories, good for snacking, and rich in vitamins."

Jolphai Maas

Ingredients:
Fish, Indian olives, turmeric, salt.

Process:
"Boil fish with Indian olives, turmeric, and salt."

Benefits:
"Rich in antioxidants and omega-3, supports heart and brain health."

Aloo Khar

Ingredients:
Potatoes, soda, turmeric, salt.

Process:
"Boil potatoes with soda, turmeric, and salt."

Benefits:
"Promotes digestion and helps cleanse the body"
