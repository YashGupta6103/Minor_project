Our Karnataka recipes are crafted to nourish both body and soul, capturing the warmth and strength of karnataka’s culinary soul.

Bisi Bele Bath

Ingredients:
Rice, toor dal, tamarind, vegetables, and spices.

Process:
"Cook rice and dal, mix with tamarind, vegetables, and a special spice mix."

Benefits:
"A balanced meal rich in protein, fiber, and carbohydrates."

Mysore Pak

Ingredients:
Gram flour, sugar, and ghee.

Process:
"Cook gram flour with sugar and ghee until it solidifies."

Benefits:
"A rich, energy-dense sweet, famous for its melt-in-the-mouth texture."

Nuchinunde

Ingredients:
Toor dal, coconut, green chilies, and curry leaves.

Process:
"Shape dal mixture into dumplings, steam until cooked."

Benefits:
"High in protein, light and healthy snack."




Chitranna

Ingredients:
Rice, lemon, green chilies, and curry leaves.

Process:
"Mix cooked rice with lemon juice, temper with spices."

Benefits:
"Tangy, refreshing, and light on the stomach."

Ragi Mudde

Ingredients:
Finger millet flour and water.

Process:
"Cook millet flour with water, shape into balls, and serve hot."

Benefits:
"High in calcium, iron, and fiber, excellent for bone health and digestion."

Upittu

Ingredients:
Semolina, vegetables, and curry leaves.

Process:
"Roast semolina, cook with sautéed vegetables, and spices."

Benefits:
"Quick, nutritious breakfast, rich in energy."

Neer Dosa

Ingredients:
Rice and water.

Process:
"Grind soaked rice into a thin batter, make thin crepes on a tawa."

Benefits:
"Light, gluten-free, and easy to digest."

Haalu Obbattu

Ingredients:
Maida, milk, jaggery, and coconut.

Process:
"Stuff sweetened milk mixture into dough, roll, and cook on a tawa."

Benefits:
"Sweet, rich in calcium, and festive treat."

Soppu Saaru

Ingredients:
Mixed greens, tamarind, and spices.

Process:
"Cook greens with tamarind."
"blend into a soup with spices."

Benefits:
"Rich in iron, vitamins, and aids digestion."



Tambuli

Ingredients:
Buttermilk, coconut, and spices.

Process:
"Blend coconut with spices, mix with buttermilk, and serve chilled."

Benefits:
"Cooling and probiotic-rich, aids digestion."

Maddur Vada

Ingredients:
Rice flour, maida, onions, and curry leaves.

Process:
"Mix ingredients into a dough, shape, and deep fry until crispy."

Benefits:
"Crispy snack, rich in flavor and texture."

Mosaru Avalakki

Ingredients:
Poha, curd, green chilies, and mustard seeds.

Process:
"Soak poha, mix with curd and temper with mustard seeds and chilies."

Benefits:
"Cooling and light, easy to digest."

Akki Shavige

Ingredients:
Rice flour, water, and salt.

Process:
"Prepare rice dough, press into noodles, and steam."

Benefits:
"Gluten-free, light, and easily digestible."

Kharasandige

Ingredients:
Rice flour, green chilies, and spices.

Process:
"Mix ingredients into a dough, shape into papads, sun-dry, and fry."

Benefits:
"Crispy snack, great with meals."

Muddu Palya

Ingredients:
Toor dal, greens (like spinach), tamarind, and spices.

Process:
"Cook dal with greens, tamarind, and spices, serve with rice."

Benefits:
"Nutritious and hearty, rich in protein and iron."
