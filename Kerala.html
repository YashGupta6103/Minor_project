Taste the true Kerala, where every recipe is steeped in history, crafted with skill, and offered with open arms.




Appam

Ingredients:
Rice flour, coconut milk, and yeast.

Process:
"Ferment rice batter, cook in a special pan for soft, lacy pancakes."

Benefits:
"Light and gluten-free, perfect with stews."

Nadan Mutta Curry

Ingredients:
Eggs, coconut milk, tomatoes, and spices.

Process:
"Boil eggs, cook in a rich coconut-based curry."

Benefits:
"Protein-rich and creamy, pairs well with parotta or rice."

Theeyal

Ingredients:
Vegetables, roasted coconut, and tamarind.

Process:
"Cook vegetables in a tangy roasted coconut gravy."

Benefits:
"Deeply flavorful, rich in antioxidants."

Ada Pradhaman

Ingredients:
Rice flakes, jaggery, and coconut milk.

Process:
"Cook rice flakes in jaggery and coconut milk, simmer until thick."

Benefits:
"Traditional festive dessert, rich and indulgent."

Pathiri

Ingredients:
Rice flour and water.

Process:
"Knead rice dough, roll into flatbreads, cook on tawa."

Benefits:
"Light and gluten-free, perfect for pairing with curries."

Kanji

Ingredients:
Rice, water, and salt.

Process:
"Boil rice until soft, add salt and serve with side dishes."

Benefits:
"Easily digestible, soothing meal often eaten during illnesses."

Vendakka Mezhukkupuratti

Ingredients:
Okra, coconut oil, and spices.

Process:
"Stir-fry okra with spices until crispy."

Benefits:
"High in fiber, light and flavorful side dish."

Chemmeen Ularthiyathu

Ingredients:
Prawns, coconut oil, curry leaves, and spices.

Process:
"Sauté prawns with spices until roasted."

Benefits:
"Omega-3 rich and flavorful, pairs well with rice."

Avocado Pachadi

Ingredients:
Avocado, yogurt, and mustard seeds.

Process:
"Blend avocado with yogurt, temper with mustard seeds."

Benefits:
"Creamy, heart-healthy, and cooling."

Kappa Vevichathu

Ingredients:
Tapioca, coconut, and green chilies.

Process:
"Boil and mash tapioca, mix with spiced coconut paste."

Benefits:
"Filling and high in carbohydrates, often paired with fish curry."

Mambazha Pulissery

Ingredients:
Ripe mango, yogurt, and coconut.

Process:
"Cook mango with coconut paste, add yogurt for a tangy taste."

Benefits:
"Cooling and rich in vitamins, unique sweet-tangy flavor."

Kutti Pathiri

Ingredients:
Rice flour, coconut, and jaggery.

Process:
"Layer rice dough and sweet coconut, steam until set."

Benefits:
"Light and naturally sweet, a unique traditional snack."

Thalassery Biryani

Ingredients:
Short-grain rice, chicken, and spices.

Process:
"Cook spiced chicken and layer with cooked rice, steam together."

Benefits:
"Rich in flavors and a complete meal."

Tapioca Chips

Ingredients:
Tapioca, coconut oil, and salt.

Process:
"Thinly slice tapioca, deep fry in coconut oil until crispy."

Benefits:
"Crispy and filling, traditional Kerala snack."

Mutta Roast
