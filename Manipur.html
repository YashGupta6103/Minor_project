Get Food recipe of Manipur
from the Food Explorer.

In Manipur cuisine, food is connection—a gathering of flavors, stories, and people brought together by a shared love for spice.

Eromba

Ingredients:
Boiled vegetables, fermented fish (ngari), and chilies.

Process:
"Mash boiled vegetables with fermented fish and chilies."

Benefits:
"High in fiber and probiotics, a flavorful and nutritious dish."

Hawaijar with Fish

Ingredients:
Fermented soybeans (hawaijar), fish, and spices.

Process:
"Cook fish with fermented soybeans and spices until well blended."

Benefits:
"High in protein and probiotics, a flavorful dish."

Paknam

Ingredients:
Rice flour, fermented fish, vegetables, and herbs.

Process:
"Mix rice flour with vegetables and fermented fish."
"steam in banana leaves."

Benefits:
"Gluten-free and rich in fiber, a healthy snack."

Chak Mangsha

Ingredients:
Black rice, meat (pork or chicken), and spices.

Process:
"Cook black rice with spiced meat and herbs."

Benefits:
"Rich in protein and antioxidants, a hearty meal."

Kanghou

Ingredients:
Vegetables, spices, and mustard oil.

Process:
"Stir-fry seasonal vegetables with mustard oil and spices."

Benefits:
"Rich in vitamins and fiber, a quick side dish."





Fish Pakora

Ingredients:
Fish, chickpea flour, and spices.

Process:
"Coat fish in chickpea flour batter and deep fry until crispy."

Benefits:
"High in protein, a crispy and flavorful snack."

Methi with Taro

Ingredients:
Fenugreek leaves, taro, and spices.

Process:
"Cook fenugreek leaves with taro and spices until soft."

Benefits:
"High in nutrients and good for digestion."

Soibum Eromba

Ingredients:
Fermented bamboo shoots, boiled vegetables, and chilies.

Process:
"Mash boiled vegetables with fermented bamboo shoots and chilies."

Benefits:
"High in fiber and antioxidants, aids in digestion."




Mayo Roti

Ingredients:
Wheat flour, water, and salt.

Process:
"Make a dough, roll into flatbreads, and cook on a hot pan."

Benefits:
"High in carbohydrates, a staple food."

Ngakra

Ingredients:
Mutton, potatoes, and spices.

Process:
"Cook mutton with potatoes and spices until tender."

Benefits:
"High in protein, a hearty and flavorful curry."

Aloo Eromba

Ingredients:
Boiled potatoes, ngari, and chilies.

Process:
"Mash boiled potatoes with ngari and add chopped chilies."

Benefits:
"High in carbohydrates, a simple and tasty dish."


Singju with Fish

Ingredients:
Mixed vegetables, fish, and groundnuts.

Process:
"Toss vegetables with fish and roasted groundnuts for a crunchy salad."

Benefits:
"High in vitamins and healthy fats, a refreshing dish."

Raja Mashi

Ingredients:
Cucumber, mustard oil, and spices.

Process:
"Cook cucumber with mustard oil and spices until tender."

Benefits:
"Hydrating and low in calories, a refreshing dish."

Kangshoi with Carrot

Ingredients:
Carrots, onions, and spices.

Process:
"Boil carrots with onions and spices until tender."

Benefits:
"Rich in beta-carotene and a healthy soup."



Methi with Boiled Rice

Ingredients:
Fenugreek leaves, boiled rice, and spices.

Process:
"Cook fenugreek leaves with spices and serve over boiled rice."

Benefits:
"Rich in iron and fiber, a nutritious meal."
