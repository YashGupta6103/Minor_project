Enjoy the traditional tastes of Andhra Pradesh, with recipes that showcase the region’s love for bold, spicy flavors.

Vegetable Sandwich

Ingredients:
Bread, cucumber, tomato, onion
Green chutney, butter, salt, pepper

Process:
"Spread butter and chutney on bread."
"Add veggies, season, and close sandwich."

Benefits:
"Full of fiber and vitamins."

Poha

Ingredients:
1 cup flattened rice (poha)
1 small onion, green chili, curry leaves
Peanuts, turmeric, salt

Process:
"Rinse poha and drain."
"Sauté peanuts, onions, curry leaves, and turmeric."
"Mix in poha and cook for 2-3 mins."

Benefits:
"Light, iron-rich, easy to digest."

Bread Omelette

Ingredients:
2 eggs, 2 bread slices
Onion, tomato, green chili
Salt, pepper

Process:
"Mix eggs, veggies, salt, and pepper."
"Cook on a pan with bread slices on top."

Benefits:
"Protein-rich, quick energy from bread."

Besan Chilla

Ingredients:
1 cup besan, onion, tomato
Green chili, turmeric, salt

Process:
"Mix all ingredients with water to make batter."
"Pour on a pan and cook both sides."

Benefits:
"Protein-packed, filling and healthy."

Bhel Puri

Ingredients:
Puffed rice, onion, tomato
Green chutney, tamarind chutney, sev

Process:
"Mix puffed rice, veggies, and chutneys."
"Add sev, serve immediately."

Benefits:
"Low-calorie, high in fiber."

Masala Bhutta

Ingredients:
Corn on the cob
Lemon, chili powder, salt

Process:
"Roast corn over a flame."
"Rub lemon, chili powder, and salt."

Benefits:
"High in fiber and antioxidants."

Fruit Chaat

Ingredients:
Chopped fruits
Lemon juice, chaat masala

Process:
"Mix fruits with lemon juice and chaat masala."
"Serve fresh."

Benefits:
"Full of vitamins and antioxidants."

Schezwan Bread Toast

Ingredients:
Bread, schezwan sauce, butter

Process:
"Spread schezwan sauce on bread."
"Toast with butter on a pan."

Benefits:
"Spicy, filling, and energizing."

Paneer Salad

Ingredients:
1 tomato, chopped, lemon juice
1 cup paneer cubes, 1 small cucumber, chopped

Process:
"Mix paneer with cucumber and tomato."
"Add lemon juice and serve."

Benefits:
"Protein-rich, refreshing."

Cheese Nachos

Ingredients:
1 cup tortilla chips, ½ cup grated cheese
Salsa (optional), jalapenos (optional)

Process:
"Spread cheese over tortilla chips."
"Microwave until cheese melts, serve with salsa."

Benefits:
"Quick snack, rich in calcium. "

Egg Salad Sandwich

Ingredients:
2 boiled eggs, 2 slices of bread
1 tbsp mayonnaise, salt, and pepper

Process:
"Chop boiled eggs, mix with mayonnaise."
"Spread on bread, season, and serve."

Benefits:
"Protein-packed, easy to prepare."

Spicy Popcorn

Ingredients:
1 cup popcorn kernels, 1 tsp chili powder.
1 tbsp oil, salt.

Process:
"Pop kernels in oil, add chili powder and salt."
"Toss well and serve."

Benefits:
"Low-calorie snack, high in fiber."

Instant Noodles

Ingredients:
1 packet instant noodles, 1 cup mixed veggies.
Spices (from noodle packet), water

Process:
"Boil noodles with vegetables and spices."
"Serve hot."

Benefits:
"Quick, filling, and flavorful."

Paneer Pakora

Ingredients:
1 cup paneer, sliced.
Besan (gram flour), spices (chili powder, salt), oil.

Process:
"Coat paneer slices with besan and spices."
"Deep fry until golden."

Benefits:
"Crispy snack, high in protein."

Instant Bread Pizza

Ingredients:
2 slices of bread, ½ cup grated cheese.
Tomato ketchup, veggies (capsicum, onion).

Process:
"Spread ketchup on bread, top with veggies and cheese."
"Microwave or grill for 3 minutes."

Benefits:
"Quick and cheesy snack, rich in calcium and vitamins."
