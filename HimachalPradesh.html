Pateer

Ingredients:
Wheat flour, yeast, and fillings like radish or spinach.

Process:
"Prepare dough with filling, steam until soft and fluffy."

Benefits:
"Light and healthy, with the goodness of greens."

Babru

Ingredients:
Wheat flour, black gram dal, and spices.

Process:
"Prepare dough, stuff with black gram paste, roll and fry until golden."

Benefits:
"A savory, protein-packed snack, often enjoyed with chutney."

Maash Dal

Ingredients:
Black gram lentils, onion, garlic, and spices.

Process:
"Slow-cook lentils with spices until creamy."

Benefits:
"Rich in protein and iron, filling and nutritious."

Himachali Bhaat
